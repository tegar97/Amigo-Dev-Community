Why Amigo Community?

We believe that having a positively strong community is important for everyone. Building a strong rapport with your community peers can make a positive impact in what is going on in our career community regarding everyone's happiness. People coming together and voicing one opinion is much stronger than one solo voice standing on its own.

What you can get by joining our community

Contribute to Open Source Projects

Every member of the community will participate in the community weekly projects which means more projects and experience to your resume.

Build your Portfolio

Every member of the community will have to use everything they've learn and worked on to build their Portfolio and showcase their skills.

Build Your Network

The Community Networking gives you new possibilities for career advancement and professional growth, and it increases the likelihood that you'll be introduced to influencer or even be referred to a job.
